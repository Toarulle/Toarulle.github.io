Companions

Rakitten

Splicer Augments

Monster Trainer Abilities

Cat’s Curse

Type: Active (bonus action)
When the rakitten hits a creature with a Claw attack on its turn, it can use a bonus action to attempt to afflict the creature with the cat’s curse for 1 minute. The creature must succeed on a Charisma saving throw or be unable to benefit from spells of a level lower than the tamer’s proficiency bonus. A creature affected by this curse can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on a success.
After the rakitten has used this action, it can’t do so again for 1 minute.
The rakitten gains this improvement when it becomes a companion; it doesn’t cost an improvement.

Speed Training

Acquired: Level 2 Tamer
Type: Passive (companion)
Increase one existing speed by 15 feet up to a maximum of 150% of the creature’s base speed, rounded up to the nearest 5-foot increment*.

Growth I

Acquired: Level 3 Tamer
Type: Passive (companion)
The rakitten’s size increases to Small, its Hit Die size increases to a d6 (its hit point maximum consequently increases by 1 for each of its Hit Dice), and the damage die of its Claw attack increases to a d6.

Limited Antimagic

Acquired: Level 4 Tamer
Type: Passive (companion)
The rakitten can’t be affected or detected by spells of 1st level or lower unless it wishes to be. When the rakitten’s tamer reaches 9th, 13th, and 17th level in the tamer class, the level of spell against which the rakitten can’t be affected or detected increases to 2nd, 3rd, and 5th level, respectively.

Rakish Resilience

Acquired: Level 5 Tamer
Type: Passive (companion)
The rakitten gains a bonus to its AC equal to its Charisma modifier while it isn’t wearing armour.

Multiattack

Acquired: Level 6 Tamer
Type: Active (action)
As an action, the rakitten can make two Claw attacks.

Weavebender

Acquired: Level 7 Tamer
Type: Active (reaction)
As a reaction when a ranged spell attack or a spell with an area of effect centred on a point, targets a creature or point within 30 feet of the rakitten, the rakitten can redirect the spell. It can cause a ranged spell attack to target a different creature within 10 feet of the original target (after the attack roll is made but before the outcome is determined), or it can move the area of effect’s point of origin up to 10 feet in any direction. The rakitten can use this reaction a number of times equal to its Charisma modifier. It regains all expended uses when its tamer finishes a long rest.

Ability Boost

Acquired: Level 8 Tamer
Type: Passive (Companion)
Increase one of your companion's ability scores på 1, to maximum of 20 (CHA 15->16)

Wind Strike

Acquired: Level 9 Tamer

Growth II

Acquired: level 10 Tamer

Survival Instincts

Acquired: Level 11 Tamer
Your companion gains proficiency in one saving throw (DEX)

Sunsnacker

HP: 6d8+6=33

Growth I

Acquired: Level 1 Tamer
Type: Passive (companion)
The sunsnacker’s size increases to Small, its Hit Die size increases to a d6 (its hit point maximum consequently increases by 1 for each of its Hit Dice), and the damage die of its beak attack increases to a d8. The sunsnacker gains this improvement when it becomes a companion; it doesn’t cost an improvement.

War Training

Acquired: Level 2 Tamer
Your companion gains proficiency with one armour type or two weapons (Light Armor)

Flame Retardant

Acquired: Level 3 Tamer
Type: Passive (companion)
The sunsnacker has resistance to fire damage.

War Training

Acquired: Level 4 Tamer
Your companion gains proficiency with one armour type or two weapons (Medium Armor)

Solar Beam I

Acquired: Level 5 Tamer
Type: Active (action)
As an action, the sunsnacker can unleash a beam of radiant energy from its distended maw. Each creature in a 5-foot-wide, 30-foot-long line must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 17 (5d6) radiant damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
After the sunsnacker uses this action, it can’t do so again until its tamer finishes a short or long rest. When the sunsnacker’s tamer reaches 9th level in the tamer class, the damage of this action increases to 24 (7d6).

Multiattack

Acquired: Level 6 Tamer
Type: Active (action)
As an action, the sunsnacker can make two beak attacks.

Growth II

Acquired: Level 7 Tamer
Type: Passive (companion)
The sunsnacker’s size increases to Medium, its Hit Die size increases to a d8 (its hit point maximum consequently increases by 1 for each of its Hit Dice), and the damage die of its beak attack increases from a d8 to d10.

Toughen Up

Acquired: Level 8 Tamer
Your companion gains an additional Hit Die, increasing its hit point maximum. Increase your companion’s hit point maximum by rolling this Hit Die and adding your companion’s Constitution modifier (minimum of 0).

Sunstep

Acquired: Level 9 Tamer

Suneater

Acquired: Level 10 Tamer

Survival Instincts

Acquired: Level 11 Tamer
Your companion gains proficiency in one saving throw (DEX)
